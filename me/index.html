About Me

Self-Analysis & External Analysis

Self-Analysis

  • Strength: I am a self-motivated data lover and an aspiring data scientist who is passionate about technology, curious about the data-driven world, tenacious about solving problems and positive about life. I have a desire to learn and want to continuously improve. The process of learning, rather than the outcome, excites me. I have an enthusiasm that is contagious and I can get others excited about what I am going to do. I am fascinated by ideas and I am able to find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena. I am also futuristic that I inspire others with my visions of the future.

  • Weakness: Everyone has weaknesses and it's better to make improvements than to hide them. First, I have the so-called "Decidophobia" that sometimes I have hard time in making choices or decisions. I need a long time to think about the benefits and bad consequences for each choice as well as gather opinions from people around me. Second, I am being too nice to people sometimes. I find it hard to say no that I would still sacrifice my own time in helping others even I have an urgent deadline. Third, when I am under pressure and have a lot of things to finish, I can only be a single-threaded person, which means that I have to focus one task at a time and will not start next one until the previous one is done. Finally, I am afraid of being alone. And under that circumstances, I sometimes get distracted by things around me easily and find hard to get focused.

Congruence Analysis

Overall, there is congruence between my self-analysis and the external analysis. I agree with most people's comments. My optimism and positivity are my biggest strength, which make me easily to get along with and work with. But, I do need to get rid of the procrastination problem and learn to say "no" properly. If I could start early, I would not get stressed out at the end. I like to make plans beforehand, which probably gives other people an impression of me thinking too much before taking an action. However, making faster and meaningful decision is also what I need to work on. I will keep up with my strengths and make improvements to my weaknesses.

Feature-Benefits

Sally's strengths make her a trustable and fun person to work with in any workplace. She is actively seeking sources of learning new skills and technology and she always has passion in things that she works on. She transfers positive energy and brings collaborative culture to any group. She is planned, persistent, hard-working and strategic in achieving a goal. Sally never stops seeking ways to improve herself and she is also humble in listening to other people's advices. She is always ready to get her hands dirty in solving challenging problems.
